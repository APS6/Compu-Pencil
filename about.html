How it started

Remember Doraemon, We decided to bring his gadget Compu Pencil to reality. We started as a team of five college friends in harvard. It took some time and a lot of hardwork and research but we knew it was going to be worth it. With our passion and dedication, we were able to bring Compu Pencil to life.
